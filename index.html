Anti -Mosquito Movement

Have you experienced being late for school due to flooded streets surrounding your area? Have you found yourself gliding and falling in your muddy streets brought about by little rainfall? Have you ever imagined being confined in the hospital due to diseases brought about by mosquitoes hovering in the swamps wreathed by tall grasses and garbages? If yes, then you're in the right site!

This site provides a lot of information about mosquitos and disease caused by it. It also has an information about dengue and its symptoms to raise awareness among the people in your community. We provided an images where you can see where the mosquitoes usually thrives.

We share, because we care.
